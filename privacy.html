Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At Frisco Landscaping ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing our website or engaging our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide you with the best landscaping services possible. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and physical property address when you request a quote or contact us.
  • Project Details: Information regarding your specific landscaping needs, yard size, and preferences.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior when you visit our website (via cookies and analytics tools).

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for specific business purposes, including:

  • Providing accurate landscaping estimates and proposals.
  • Scheduling and performing maintenance services, installations, and repairs.
  • Communicating with you about your appointments, invoices, and service updates.
  • Improving our website functionality and customer service experience.
  • Sending promotional emails or newsletters (you may opt-out at any time).

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses "cookies" to enhance your experience. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device to help us analyze web traffic and remember your preferences. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. However, declining cookies may prevent you from taking full advantage of the website.

5. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security, as no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
